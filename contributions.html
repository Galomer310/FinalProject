Key Contributions to Science and Technology

AC Electrical Systems: Tesla’s development of alternating current (AC) power systems is one of the most significant achievements in electrical engineering.

Radio and Wireless Technology: His pioneering work in radio transmission and wireless communication laid the groundwork for many modern technologies.

Nikola Tesla's legacy as one of the most influential inventors and engineers in history is firmly established. His visionary ideas and groundbreaking inventions continue to inspire scientists, engineers, and technologists around the world.

Electromagnetism and Power Transmission: Tesla’s inventions and theoretical work in electromagnetism continue to influence electrical engineering and power transmission technologies.
